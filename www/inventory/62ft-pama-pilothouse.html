The PAMA 620LX Pilothouse has garnered rave reviews for quality, functional innovations, impressive standard outfit, and an incomparably elegant interior.

Accommodations include 3 luxurious staterooms all appointed with high-end finishes and 2 large heads with stall showers, marble countertops and floors. A pilothouse with a center pilot station, 2 Stidd helm seats, a forward facing settee, u-shaped open galley, port & stbd side doors and stairs leading to the bridge. The salon is open & spacious and offers a 42' flat screen television w/Sat TV system, large sofa and two comfortable barrel chairs. The bridge offers 2 Stidd chairs, settee and an u-shaped bar/food entertainment center.

And this 2005 Pama 620 LX "Far Niente" is no exception. Highlighting "Far Niente's" distinctions include reliable MTU Series 60 825hp at 2300 RPM mains, 2.5:1 ratio gearboxes, Northern Lights 20 kw genset with air/water separator, Sidepower SP220TC 24v 15 hp bow thruster, Prosine & Magnum inverter systems, Battery charger, New battery system, Kabola hydronic diesel heat system, Air-conditioning/heat climate systems with control panels, Furuno Navnet and full navigation system, Seatel 2498S satellite TV antenna system, Central vac system with hose and accessories, Washer/dryer, Starboard side aft control aft station, Dripless shaft seals, Windlass, SeaRecovery Aquatic SRC 900-2 AMC watermaker, Glendinning 50amp cablemaster, Steelhead SMI1500 yacht crane and Nautica 15 WB center console tender with Yamaha 60hp 4 stroke outboard.
