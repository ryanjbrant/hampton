Audre Grace is accessed from a teak swimstep with a Euro style transom leading to the raised teak aft deck and full walk around side decks forward.. The salon is beamy and open with a formal dining area forward. New carpet, wood décor blinds, custom oversized sofa and two leather Stressless recliners make this area comfortable and inviting. The pilothouse has a large L-lounge with a table to starboard, and an open galley with a cooktop island, oven, full-sized refrigerator and plenty of storage. Accommodations includes 4 staterooms and 4 heads. Flybridge access is easy from either the pilothouse or aft deck stairs.

Audre Grace has much to offer at a very attractive value.
