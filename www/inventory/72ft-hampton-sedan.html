The Hampton 720 Motoryacht was designed for discerning owners whom are looking for a level of luxury that is not commonly found in this size class. The yacht is fitted with sumptuous interior appointments and top-of-the line mechanical equipment, which combined makes for a gorgeous motoryacht that can take you on the voyage of your dreams. She is beautiful, yet rugged and with her wide beam creates very accommodating staterooms and cabins. The use of quality soft-goods with Granite, Marble & Onyx combined and our satin finished raised panel Makore Cherry creates stunning staterooms & a main salon that our owners are proud to show their friends.

The H720 was designed for those individuals who want a world class cruising yacht that is easily operated by a couple. The lists of amenities are long and the excuses are short… We build the finest yachts for discriminating individuals….

Our Hampton series embodies decades of boat building knowledge combined with today’s technology which has enabled Hampton Yachts to produce the perfect ocean going cruising yacht that meets the stringent demands of our knowledgeable customers. With Hampton’s quality building practices and our trademark of luxurious amenities, exotic woods and flexible interior designs our customers can create their ultimate cruising yacht.

Luxury is the most appropriate word to begin to describe this absolutely gorgeous motoryacht. The custom designer interior offers sumptous fabrics designed to be both pleasing to the eye and soft to the touch. Accommodations start with a beautifully designed 5 stateroom, 4 bath interior that is finished with “raised panel” Makore Cherry & inlaid "booked matched" Redwood burl.

The "Full Beam Owners stateroom" is located midships, is beautifully appointed and features a "King" size bed, walk-in clothes locker and a head with Onyx countertops & floors and a large shower. Other notable items include additional clothes lockers, a portside settee, vanity, custom wall treatments; TV & Stereo with surround sound system, numerous storage drawers and a custom designed headliner with indirect lighting.

"Owners Bath/Head" features includes Onyx countertops & floors, Tecma toilet, LED task lighting and a large stall shower.

The "VIP stateroom" is an open and sumptuous with gorgeous soft-goods and bedding that creates a private getaway that might entice your guests to never leave their room. The stateroom offers a queen size bed, skylight with Ocean-Air screen system, TV w/Stereo CD/DVD system, hanging locker & drawer storage, a vanity and an ensuite head.

The “VIP Bath/Head” features Onyx countertops & floors, Tecma toilet, LED lighting and a large stall shower.

The "Office/3rd stateroom" features a single settee, bureau w/drawers, LED lighting, TV, Stereo/CD/DVD system, clothes locker, drawer storage and an ensuite head.

The "Office/3rd strm Bath/Head" features Onyx countertop & floor, Techa toilet, LED task lighting, stall shower, storage cabinet & port light with screen.

The “Crew Quarters” is accessed from the main salon or through the transom watertight door. The space features (2) crew cabins, head w/shower, washer & dryer and a galley. Coming down from the salon there is a privacy door at the base of the stairway to keep the crew area separate from the owner’s spaces.

The “1st crew stateroom” features a single berth, hanging locker & drawer storage, LED task lighting, port light with screens and a TV with Stereo/CD/DVD system.

The “2nd crew stateroom” features a single berth, hanging locker & drawer storage, portlight with screen, LED task lighting and stereo system.

The "Salon" was designed to create an inviting living space that gives it owners & guests an unobstructed 360 degree view to enjoy the view whether cruising or at dockside. Some of the salon features include custom designed furniture, raised panel cherry cabinetry, custom coffee table, boxed windows w/Hunter-Douglas blinds, bar w/icemaker, 42" LED TV, Bose surround sound system, LED lighting w/dimmers and a custom built formal dining table.

The "Lower helm station" features a center helm with full navigation & engine instrumentation, (2) Pompanette helm chairs, port & starboard "aircraft style" deck access doors.

Galley The galley on the Hampton 720 is located in the crew area to keep the salon space open for entertaining. The galley is large, features quality cooking equipment and has a serving window, so when the owners are entertaining they can keep the galley area private and still enable the chef to pass food through to stewards.

The galley offers Corian countertops & flooring, abundant storage; Appliances are Bocsh and include Refrigerator & freezer, dishwasher, Ceramic cooktop, 24” Oven and a Microwave. Other equipment includes LED lighting, range hood, Garbage disposal, Grohe faucet and a large double stainless steel sink.
